Sasanian Empire:224,651:The Sasanian Empire was the last pre-Islamic Persian empire.
Tang Dynasty:618,907:The Tang Dynasty is considered a golden age of Chinese arts and culture.
Abbasid Caliphate:750,1258:The Abbasid Caliphate, with the capital city in Baghdad, is considered the Islamic Golden Age fostering great cultural and scientific growth.
Pagan Kingdom:849,1297:Pagan Kingdom was the first Burmese kingdom to unify the regions that would later constitute modern-day Myanmar.
Mongol Empire:1206,1368:The Mongol Empire of the 13th and 14th centuries was the largest contiguous empire in history.
Khmer Empire:802,1431:The Khmer Empire was a Hindu-Buddhist empire in Southeast Asia, centered around hydraulic cities in what is now northern Cambodia.
Roman Empire:-27,1453:The Roman Empire was the largest and most powerful political and military entity in the world.
Byzantine Empire:395,1453:The Byzantine Empire, also referred to as the Eastern Roman Empire, was the continuation of the Roman Empire with the capital city set in Constantinople.
Inca Empire:1438,1533:The Inca Empire was the largest ancient civilization in pre-Columbian America.
